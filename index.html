How do phase-adjusted odds work?

Each bet can have a phase tag that shifts its odds depending on where the episode falls in the season (early ← mid → late). The season is split into three thirds based on an estimated ~28-episode horizon (adapts if more episodes are added).

Phase tagEarly ep.Mid ep.Late ep.Idea
early-heavy×0.7×1.2×1.8Likely early on, pays more later
mid-peak×1.3×0.8×1.3Most likely mid-season, cheaper then
late-heavy×1.8×1.2×0.7Unlikely early, common in the finale stretch
any×1.0×1.0×1.0No adjustment — flat odds all season

Example: A bet with base odds 3.0× tagged early-heavy added to Episode 2 (early) becomes 3.0 × 0.7 = 2.1×. The same bet in Episode 20 (late) becomes 3.0 × 1.8 = 5.4×. You can override the phase on any bet or set it to any to disable the adjustment.
